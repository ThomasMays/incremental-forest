(Quick) About

Start with nothing but an axe, chop down some trees, sell logs, and hire workers to do stuff for you.

Controls

  • Movement: Arrows or WASD
  • Interact with objects / Use selected item: SPACE
  • Select item slot: Numbers 1 - 8
  • Sell an item: Right-Click on the item in your inventory

How To Play

  • Walk up to a tree and press SPACE several times to chop it down.
    (You will be able to buy an upgraded axe later in the game's devlopment to make chopping trees easier.)
  • Collect the item(s) the tree dropped.
  • Sell the log.
  • If you didn't sell the pine cone, you can plant it by pressing SPACE on an empty tile.
  • After you've made some money, hire a worker. They'll get to work right away.
  • When you don't need a worker anymore, fire them.
  • If you hire a Collector, don't forget to sell the logs they pick up for you.

Workers

  • You must pay a deposit to hire a worker (different workers have different deposit amounts)
  • Workers charge a small fee whenever
  • Choppers will chop down trees
    Deposit: $150
  • Collectors will collect items on the ground and magically place them in your inventory
    Deposit: $50
    • If you can't hold anymore of an item, Collectors will just stand in front of the item trying to pick it up (bug)
  • Planters will magically take pine cones from your inventory and plant them in inconvenient places
    Deposit: $75
    • They also like to trap themselves and/or other workers among the trees they planted

Shortcuts

For keyboard people like myself.

  • Pause/Resume the game: P
  • Previous / Next item slot: Q / E
  • Sell selected item: L
  • Sell all of selected item: SHIFT + L/Right-Click
  • Hire selected worker type: H
  • Fire selected worker type: F

Other Notes

  • There are no mobile controls at the moment. If anyone has any ideas for how the controls should work let me know and I'll add them.
  • Workers are pretty dumb at the moment.
  • The game will pause if the page is not visible.
  • You'll eventually run out of trees and pine cones.
  • There are more item slots than there are items in the game.
  • Water is currently useless. It's from an old idea for the game. It says you can collect it but you can't unless you have a bucket (which you can't acquire).
